Bridal Collection
Individually Numbered

Love Art Wear Art is proud to present its new, limited edition wedding collection. A capsule range of beautiful, luxurious, hand made shoes to finish your perfect day with the perfect pair of shoes. Feel feminine and elegant in this range of vintage inspired courts, sandals & ankle boots. All our shoes are part of limited runs with only 150 pairs made per style to ensure a very unique shoe for your wedding day.

The range is made from the highest quality kid suede, luxury satin, metallic and glitter finished leather. All shoes are leather lined with an embossed natural leather outsole. The attention to detail is exquisite and each pair is individually numbered with a tiny metal banner on the breast of the sole.

We stock UK sizes 4-7 in all the above styles but please contact us directly if you have a special request or would like more info on your favourite style. All the prices are available on request.

We are taking direct orders, please contact Nicole at  info@loveartwearart.com or shop through one of our stockists below.

Try before you buy

Not 100% sure about fit, style or colour? We know how important it is that your shoes are the perfect match to your dress. The best way to judge this is to try them together. So we are happy to offer a ‘try before you buy’ option so you can try our lovely shoes in the comfort of your home.

For a pre pay fee we can send you a half pair in your selected style & size. We sent the shoes through secure, special delivery (1 working day within the UK) and a free post label for the return of the samples. Please contact us for futher information.

You will receive a full refund when you purchase your pair of shoes.

This service is only available within the UK. Payment accepted through Paypal or bank transfer.
