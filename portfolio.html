Nicole le Grange Portfolio

Hello and thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio.

My name is Nicole le Grange, I am a ladies footwear designer with over 10 years experience. I started working on the supplier side for BSV and Peter Black and moved to retail as the senior designing at Dorothy Perkins. I am the cofounder of a bespoke bridal and occasion footwear label, Love Art Wear Art and I have been working as a freelance designer for the past 5 years. My clients include well known retailers such as Marks & Spencers, Topshop, Accesorize & New Look.

I have worked across all of ladies footwear in my design career but with a focus on occasion & fast fashion. I have a very feminine and commercial hand writing with a detailed hand drawing style. I am very proficient in Photoshop & Illustrator and I have a lot of experience working with factories & samples rooms in China, India, Spain & Italy.

I am a very motivated individual, I enjoy taking on new challenges and I am currently looking for my next career move. I am interested in senior footwear design opportunities. Below you will find a few samples of my recent work for Topshop, M&S and Accesorize.
