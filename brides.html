We've been creating our gorgeous bridal shoes for just over a year now. I am very excited that we can finally start looking back at some of the amazing weddings over the past season that Love Art Wear Art played a tiny role in! We would like to share a few snapshots of some of our lovely brides, their grooms & their wedding days. It is so nice to see all the detailed plans come together on the day. For me it's very special when someone chooses our shoes to finish of their outfit. So its with pride and joy that we share these lovely moments with you.

Danielle & Josh

Danielle and Josh had their wedding in the green and leafy Greenwhich. Danielle wore a beautiful beaded gown by Charlotte Wilden Bride. She teamed this elegant dress with our Anoush white & silver suede platform peep toe sandals.

Danielle: "I really struggled to find anything that I wanted to wear let alone that I could justify paying paying the sometimes eye watering prices for however in my extensive search I came across a photo shoot and loved the shoes on the model. I was therefore delighted when I found that not only was I able to find them online, the price was reasonable. As much as I needed my shoes to have as high a heel as possible (my husband is 6 foot 8) I was taking the 'bury my head in the sand' approach to how much my feet were going to ache wearing them however I can honestly say that these shoes were the most comfortable heels I have ever worn. I managed 11am to 10pm which to me makes them worth every penny. Thank you so much Nicole, I look forward to finding any excuse possible to get them out again!”
