About Love Art Wear Art

Love Art Wear Art creates beautiful, limited edition, handmade shoes for shoe lovers that appreciate quality & uniqueness.

 

The Love Art Wear Art collection is a luxury footwear line that offers limited edition shoes to the discerning boutique market. LAWA aims to marry the spirit of glamorous eras gone by with a unique contemporary twist introduced through subtle detail and tasteful styling. Love Art Wear Art shoes are modern, unique pieces that don't follow trends. It's timeless, elegant shoes that will last for season to come. Textures & colour is key with an exciting use of suede, leather, stand out metallics, foil printed suedes, die cut leather and metal hardware details.

 

Born in South Africa, Nicole le Grange is a British footwear designer, based in London. A graduate of Cordwainers, London College of Fashion, Nicole has worked with a number of British designers and well known retailers. In February 2011 she launched her own collection of luxury women's shoes. Love Art Wear Art's limited edition shoes are designed in London and manufactured in Spain by a prestigious, family run factory in Elda.

We produce limited runs of each style & colour so that we can guarantee you will be wearing something unique. Each pair is individually numbered on a little metal banner on the breast of the embossed leather outsole.

Nicole Le Grange, trading as Love Art Wear Art, won the designer award at the Footwear Friends Annual Awards 2012. She collaborated with designer Nico Didonna on his London Fashion Week show in September 2013. Love Art Wear Art is a very exciting young label to watch, set to be a fashion footwear staple.
