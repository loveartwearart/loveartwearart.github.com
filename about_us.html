About Love Art Wear Art

Love Art Wear Art creates beautiful, limited edition, handmade shoes for shoe lovers that appreciate quality & uniqueness.

 

Designed in London and produced in Spain, Love Art Wear Art is known for its gorgeous, individually numbered ladies shoes. Love Art Wear Art is a luxury footwear label and supplies limited edition shoes to the discerning boutique market. The shoes are feminine, contemporary and commercial with sharp styling and intricate detailing, LAWA aims to marry the spirit of glamorous eras gone by with a unique contemporary twist.

All Love Art Wear Art shoes are created as part of limited ranges. We produce limited runs of each style & colour so that we can guarantee you will be wearing something unique. Each pair is individually numbered on a little metal banner on the breast of the embossed leather outsole.

Armed with an undisputable talent for design, a keen eye for detail & embellishment and natural creativity, South African born Nicole le Grange creates her luxurious collection of limited edition, beautiful and unique footwear. Love Art Wear Art shoes offer a combination of uniqueness, comfort and beautiful design.


Have a look around our world and if you love our art, wear it!
