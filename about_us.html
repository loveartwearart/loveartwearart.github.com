About Love Art Wear Art

Love Art Wear Art creates beautiful, limited edition, handmade shoes for shoe lovers that appreciate quality & uniqueness.

The Love Art Wear Art collection is a luxury bridal footwear collection that offers limited edition shoes to the discerning bridal market. LAWA aims to marry the spirit of glamorous eras gone by with a unique contemporary twist introduced through subtle detail and tasteful styling. LAWA shoes are timeless, elegant and unique pieces that don't follow trends. Textures & luxury materials are key. Our styles incorporate a playful mix of Italian & Spanish sourced satin, kid skin, metallic leather, glitter leather and metal hardware details.

Your comfort is very important to us, we want to make sure our brides can wear their shoes all day long. We put an extra layer of rubber foam on all our insoles to offer a soft padded sock. The shoes are leather lined so moves and breathes with you. Each pair is hand stitched and lasted in Spain. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, quality and excellent customer care, so get in touch to find your perfect pair!

Born in South Africa, Nicole le Grange is a British footwear designer, based in London. A graduate of Cordwainers, London College of Fashion, Nicole has worked with a number of British designers and well known retailers. She established in Love Art Wear Art bridal in 2013 and it continues to be at the forefront of the independent designer shoe and accessory retailing in the UK. With only 150 pairs made per style and colour it offers a very unique option to the discerning bride.

We produce limited runs of each style & colour so that we can guarantee you will be wearing something unique. Each pair is individually numbered on a little metal banner on the breast of the embossed leather outsole. Love Art Wear Art is a very exciting young label to watch, set to be a bridal footwear staple.
