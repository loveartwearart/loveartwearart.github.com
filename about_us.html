About Love Art Wear Art

Love Art Wear Art creates beautiful, limited edition, handmade shoes for shoe lovers that appreciate quality & uniqueness.

The Love Art Wear Art collection is a luxury footwear that offers limited edition shoes to the discerning boutique market. LAWA aims to marry the spirit of glamorous eras gone by with a unique contemporary twist introduced through subtle detail and tasteful styling. Love Art Wear Art shoes are modern, unique pieces that don't follow trends. It is timeless, elegant styles that will be perfect for season to come. Textures & colour is key with an exciting use of suede, leather, stand out metallics, glitter fabric, die cut leather and metal hardware details.

Born in South Africa, Nicole le Grange is a British footwear designer, based in London. A graduate of Cordwainers, London College of Fashion, Nicole has worked with a number of British designers and well known retailers. Established in 2012, Love Art Wear Art Bridal continues to be at the forefront of the independent designer shoe and accessory retailing in the UK. The limited edition designs are playful and elegant. With only 150 pairs made per style and colour it offers a very unique option to the discerning bride. The shoes are designed in London and hand made in Spain. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, quality and excellent customer care, so get in touch to find your perfect pair!

We produce limited runs of each style & colour so that we can guarantee you will be wearing something unique. Each pair is individually numbered on a little metal banner on the breast of the embossed leather outsole. Love Art Wear Art is a very exciting young label to watch, set to be a bridal footwear staple.
